# Telnyx 10DLC Campaign Copy

Use this copy to avoid the rejection shown in the screenshots. The key correction is that the opt-in workflow uses one specific method only: the public website SMS consent form.

## Campaign description

Essayons Bax Recovery & Logistics uses SMS messaging to communicate with drivers, dispatch personnel, contractors, and logistics staff regarding route assignments, pickup schedules, dispatch notifications, operational updates, delivery confirmations, schedule changes, driver packet notices, and logistics coordination. Messages are sent only to users who opt in through the website SMS consent form.

## Use case

Customer Care

## Message flow

Users opt in only by visiting the SMS consent form at https://essayonsbax.com/sms-consent.html. The form explains the SMS program and requires the user to enter their mobile phone number and check an SMS consent checkbox before submission. The checkbox states that the user expressly consents to receive SMS text messages from Essayons Bax Recovery & Logistics for dispatch notifications, route assignments, pickup updates, delivery updates, operational communications, schedule changes, driver packet notifications, and logistics support messages. The form also states that message frequency varies, message and data rates may apply, users may reply STOP to opt out, users may reply HELP for assistance, and consent is not a condition of employment or service.
