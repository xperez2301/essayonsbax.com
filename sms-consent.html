SMS Dispatch Notification Consent

Drivers, contractors, and logistics personnel may use this form to opt in to SMS communications from Essayons Bax Recovery & Logistics.

By submitting this form, you acknowledge that you have reviewed our Privacy Policy, Terms and Conditions, and SMS Terms.

How Consent Is Collected

Mobile numbers are collected through this online SMS Consent Form. Individuals voluntarily provide their full name and mobile phone number and must actively check the consent checkbox before receiving SMS communications.

Verbal Consent Script

"By providing your mobile phone number, you agree to receive SMS dispatch notifications, route assignments, pickup updates, delivery updates, and operational communications from Essayons Bax Recovery & Logistics. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out and HELP for assistance. Do you consent to receive these text messages?"
