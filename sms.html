SMS Terms

Effective Date: June 18, 2026

Program Description

Essayons Bax Recovery & Logistics may send SMS messages for dispatch updates, route assignments, driver packet notifications, schedule changes, pickup coordination, delivery updates, and operational support.

Consent

By submitting the SMS Consent Form and checking the consent box, you agree to receive SMS messages from Essayons Bax Recovery & Logistics at the mobile number provided. Consent is not a condition of employment or service.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on dispatch activity, assigned routes, pickup coordination, schedule changes, and operational needs.

Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan.

Opt Out

You may opt out at any time by replying STOP. After you reply STOP, you may receive a confirmation message and will no longer receive SMS messages unless you opt in again.

Help

Privacy
